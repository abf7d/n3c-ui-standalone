Sample: All Patients in the N3C Data Enclave who have had a medical visit on or after 1/1/2019 in which {{ title }} was coded. Given that patients do not have a clear date on which they were diagnosed with {{ title }}, we assume the date of their first recorded medical visit coded for {{ title }} is the day they were diagnosed with {{ title }}. If a patient with {{ title }} has not been diagnosed or has not had a medical visit related to {{ title }} within their EHR, they would be missing from this data. Patient counts in relation to COVID-19 diagnosis are calculated using the number of days between their earliest recorded medical visit coded for {{ title }} and their   diagnosis.
