Privacy-Preserving Record Linkage

The Linkage Honest Broker (LHB) PPRL dashboard is a current representation of submission by data contributing sites and the total number of matches between N3C and other data sets as well as multiple enclaves. The data are refreshed every Monday. Numbers may change as additional data is received. Further details and explanations about the data can be found by hovering your cursor over portions of the dashboard.
